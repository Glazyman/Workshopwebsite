Common questions

Straight answers.

What does the workshop actually look like?

A 45–60 minute live event held at your clinic or a nearby venue. Your team presents to a small group of pre-screened prospects using the script and slide outline we provide. There's a live Q&A to surface and handle objections, a clear offer at the end with a same-day incentive (the Golden Ticket), and a one-on-one close with every attendee before they leave. Think lunch-and-learn — your clinic as the trusted expert, the attendees as pre-interested prospects who showed up voluntarily.

Who presents at the workshop — us or you?

Your team presents. We build everything for you — the script, the slides, the offer framework — but the clinic's doctor, clinic manager, or a designated presenter runs the room. Why? Because your prospects need to trust your clinic, not a third party. We're behind the scenes making it work. We do a full prep call with your presenter before the event.

How many people typically attend?

We target 10–20 qualified attendees. More than 20 makes the room close harder to manage; fewer than 10 makes the math harder. We build the ad campaign to fill that window and give you a realistic expectation before your event date is set — not after.

Do you guarantee results?

We target closing 50% of the room and we've seen this system work across multiple medical and aesthetic markets. That said, results vary by market, how your team delivers the script, and how your offer is structured. We don't make income guarantees. What we do commit to is running the full system exactly as designed and working with you to refine based on real data from your workshop.

What does my clinic actually have to do?
  • Pick a date within 30 days of agreeing to move forward
  • Cover $300–500 in ad spend (paid upfront)
  • Provide a venue — at your clinic is fine
  • Assign a presenter and join us for a prep call
  • Have 2 staff members present on workshop day
  • Follow our checklist — we handle everything else
Why is the first one free? What's the catch?

Hair restoration is a market we're actively building this workshop model for. The first workshop is our proof of concept — for both of us. If it delivers, we continue on a paid retainer. If it doesn't, you've only spent the $300–500 in ad spend you would have put into regular ads anyway. We take the risk on our time and the full system. You cover the media cost.

What happens after the first workshop?

We debrief together — attendees, conversions, revenue generated. If the pilot hits, we agree on a monthly cadence and move to a paid engagement. The system is already built — it just gets more dialed in each time you run it.
